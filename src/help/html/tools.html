MySerieS Tools
MySerieS tools include :
  1. [Export Episodes] for exporting a series episodes to a file.
  2. [Import Episodes] to load a series episodes that were previously exported.
  3. [Download torrent] to download torrents from http://www.eztv.it or http://www.isohunt.com
  4. [Internet Update] to update series episodes information from http://www.tvrage.com or http://www.epguides.com.
  5. [Recycle Bin] to restore or totaly delete previously deleted series.
  6. [Update files] to update the download and subtitle statuses of the episodes by checking the local folder.
  7. [Housekeeping] to delete files that are needed no more.
  8. [Customize Toolbar] to add/remove buttons from the main toolbar.
