Customize Toolbar

You can customize the toolbar by right clicking on it and choose "Customize" or from the tools menu.
Then in the new panel that appears you can select which buttons you want to be visible and which you don't by drag them from the used buttons pannel and drop them to the unused buttons panel or vice versa.
You can add or remove all buttons by clicking on the or You can also drag the toolbar to the left-bottom-right side or make it float.
