The Filters panel

The filters panel show episodes from all series that are not hidden after some custom filters are applied to them.
The filters are Download status, Subtitle status and seen status.
You can save filters for later use by typing the filter name in the textbox and pressing the save button . You can also delete filters you no longer need by pressing the delete button .
From the filtered table you can edit episodes information such as download , subs and seen.
Right clicking on an episode will bring up the [Episodes pop up menu].
