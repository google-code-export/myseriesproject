MySerieS Main Window
The main window of the [Application] consists of the following panels:
  • [The Series panel:] A panel with all the series inserted in the database.
  • [The Episodes panel:] A panel with the episodes of the current selected series.
  • [The Filters panel:] A panel with the episodes of all the enabled series filtered with a custom user filter (seen or not seen, with or without subtitles etc.)
  • [The Ratings panel:] A panel with 2 tables displaying the average ratings of the series nad the episodes ratings.
  • [The Schedule panel:] A panel with a calendar displaying the schedule of the series.
  • [The Feeds panel:] A panel displaying added Rss feeds.
