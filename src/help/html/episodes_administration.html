Episodes Administration
Episodes Administrations tasks consist of:
  1. Adding an episode:
    You can add an episode in the following ways:
    • Right clicking on the [Series Panel] on the series you want to add the episode to and choose "Add a new episode to..."
    • Click on Toolbar menu Edit-Add a new episode to... after selecting a series in the [Series Panel]
    • right click on the [Episodes Panel] of the series you want to add the episode to and choose "Add a new episode to..."
    Then the Add a new episode panel will show up:

    Episode is the episode number (it's auto filled with the next episode of the series in list)
    Title is the episode's title
    Date is the episodes aired date (It's format can be defined in the [Options])
    Downloaded and seen define if the episode has been downloaded / seen and
    Subtitles it's about if subtitles have been downloaded (None - Greek, English - Both)

  2. Editing an episode:
    You can add an episode simply by double clicking on the field of the episode to edit in the [Episodes Panel] and edit it's value.

  3. Deleting an episode
    You can delete an episode by right clicking on the episode in the [Episodes Panel] and choose "Delete Episode"
