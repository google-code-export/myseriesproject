The Episodes Panel
The episodes panel consists of a table with 6 columns:
  1. The episode number: The episode number in the current season.
  2. The Episode title: The title of the episode.
  3. Aired:The date when the episode has been or will be aired. The date format is defined in the [Options].
  4. Downloaded:If the episodes has been downloaded or not.
  5. Subtitles:If subtitles have been downloaded or not.Also which subtitles has been downloaded (Greek, English or both).
  6. Seen:If the episodes has been seen or not.

To edit an episode just click on a cell and it will be converted to edit mode.
If you have given the local directory in [Add/Edit Series Panel] then the application search the series directory and updates the downloaded and subtitles info.
Also the episodes that are downloaded , have subtitles and are not seen yet are shown in bold. Right clicking on an episode will bring up the [Episodes pop up menu].
