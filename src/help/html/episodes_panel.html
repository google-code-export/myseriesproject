The Episodes Panel
The episodes panel consists of a table with 7 columns:
  1. The episode number: The episode number in the current season.
  2. The Episode title: The title of the episode.
    If an icon appears before the title means that:
    • : The episode is aired but not downloaded yet
    • : The episode's video has been downloaded but subtitles are not
    • : Episode and subtitles are downloaded but not renamed
    • : The episode's video and subtitles are downloaded and renamed.Should watch it!!!
    Double clicking on the title will open the media player for watching the episode.
  3. Aired:The date when the episode has been or will be aired. The date format is defined in the [Options].
  4. Video File:If the episodes has been downloaded or not. If the series directory is provided in [Add/Edit Series Panel] an icon of the video type is displayed. Supported video types are avi, mkv, mpg and mp4
  5. Subtitles:If subtitles have been downloaded or not.Also which subtitles has been downloaded (None, primary language, secondary language or both). Supported subtitles are srt and sub
  6. Watched:If the episodes has been watched or not.
  7. Rate:The episode's rate.You can rate only episodes that are marked as seen.
    To rate an episode double click the rate cell.

To edit an episode click on a cell , press F2 and it will be converted to edit mode.
If you have given the local directory in [Add/Edit Series Panel] then the application search the series directory and updates the downloaded and subtitles info.
Right clicking on an episode will bring up the [Episodes pop up menu].
